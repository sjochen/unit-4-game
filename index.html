Hockey Heroes!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four hockey players below. By clicking on a player you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You will win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes over the random number.


The value of each player is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each player.
